Who We Are

Redeem Christian Church of God (RCCG) His Presence Newcastle is a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spreading the gospel and experiencing God's presence. We are part of the global RCCG family, committed to holiness, evangelism, and discipleship.

Faith inspires us, Love unites us.

We are confident that God uses ordinary people to achieve extraordinary things. We believe this necessitates honesty and vulnerability within our community. This fosters an atmosphere of authenticity where individuals can flourish and their Christian faith can deepen.
